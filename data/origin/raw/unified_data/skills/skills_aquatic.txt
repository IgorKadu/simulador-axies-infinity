## Cartas de Custo 0:

### Para o Olho:
- **Clear:** If Draw Pile has at least 2 Cards, draw 2 then discard 2. Unstable.
- **Telescope:** Initial: pick a Aquatic Card from Discard Pile; otherwise, from Draw Pile. Put it on top of Draw Pile. Banish.

### Para a Cauda:
- **Nimo:** Pick 1 Nimo Card from Draw or Discard Pile then put it into Hand. Initial: gain 1 Fragment. Banish.

## Cartas de Custo 1:

### Para o Olho:
- **Gero:** 60 DMG. Ambush. Apply 3 Fragile.
  - Level 2: 70 DMG. And 3 Death Mark.
- **Sleepless:** 60 DMG. Ambush. Apply 3 Vulnerable.
  - Level 2: 70 DMG. Apply 1 Vulnerable to other enemies.

### Para a Orelha:
- **Bubblemaker:** 70 Shield. Target any ally. +1 Bulwark. +1 Bubble to allied Axies, bonus 1 Bubble per 2 Bulwark.
  - Level 2: 80 Shield.
- **Gill:** 25 DMG. Attack all enemies. +1 Bubble to allied Axies.
  - Level 2: 30 DMG. Attack all enemies. +2 Bubble to allied Axies.
- **Inkling:** 55 DMG. Ambush. Initial: +1 Jinx to Enemy Draw Pile; otherwise, to Discard Pile.
  - Level 2: 65 DMG.
- **Nimo:** 60 Shield. Target any ally. +1 Bulwark. Gain 1 Fragment per Nimo Card in your Deck. (Cap: 2 Fragments)
  - Level 2: 70 Shield.
- **Seaslug:** 65 DMG. Ambush. If debuffed, Cleanse: 4 and deal +15 DMG; otherwise, +4 Cleanser.
  - Level 2: 75 DMG.

### Para a Boca:
- **Catfish:** 25 DMG. Attack all enemies. +1 Bubble to allied Axies.
  - Level 2: 30 DMG. +2 Bubble to allied Axies.
- **Lam:** 90 DMG. Target the lowest HP enemy Axie. -25 DMG per 25% HP the target is missing.
  - Level 2: 100 DMG. Target the lowest HP enemy Axie. -15 DMG per 25% HP the target is missing.
- **Piranha:** 55 DMG. Ambush. If target isn't Alert, Steal (25% of unblocked DMG) as HP.
  - Level 2: 65 DMG.
- **Risky Fish:** 70 DMG. Initial: Trigger an effect if this card is the first card played in a turn. +30 DMG and apply 3 Bleed to self.
  - Level 2: 80 DMG. Initial: +30 DMG and apply 2 Bleed to self.

### Para a Chifre:
- **Anemone:** 80 DMG. Ambush. When played, -10 ATK. Whenever an enemy Axie dies, +20 ATK.
  - Level 2: 90 DMG.
- **Babylonia:** 60 DMG. Ambush. Apply 3 Vulnerable.
  - Level 2: 70 DMG. Ambush. Apply 3 Vulnerable and 1 Death Mark.
- **Clamshell:** 65 DMG. Gain 1 Fragment if this attack causes HP loss.
  - Level 2: 75 DMG. Gain 1 Fragment & (40% Card's ATK) as Shield if this attack causes HP loss.
- **Oranda:** 55 DMG. Target any enemy. Dispel: 5.
  - Level 2: 65 DMG. Target any enemy. Deal +5 DMG per Buff counter on the target (cap: 25 DMG) then Dispel: 5.
- **Shoal Star:** Retain. Target any enemy. On targets with <= 90 HP & 0 Shield, deal 90 DMG. If they don't die, deal 30 DMG to self.
  - Level 2: Retain. Target any enemy. On targets with <= 105 HP & 0 Shield, deal 105 DMG. If they don't die, deal 30 DMG to self.
- **Teal Shell:** 30 Shield. Target any ally. +15 Shield per other Card played this turn (cap: 90). +1 Bulwark per 45 Shield granted.
  - Level 2: 40 Shield. Target any ally. +20 Shield per other Card played this turn (cap: 100). +1 Bulwark per 40 Shield granted.

### Para a Costas:
- **Anemone:** 80 DMG. Ambush. When played, -10 ATK. Whenever an enemy Axie dies, +20 ATK.
  - Level 2: 90 DMG. Ambush.
- **Blue Moon:** 60 DMG. Ambush. Limit 3: Draw 1 card.
  - Level 2: 70 DMG. Ambush. Limit 3: Draw 1 card. Bonus 10% Stat to drawn card.
- **Goldfish:** 65 DMG. Gain 1 Fragment per Skill Card in Hand. (Cap: 2 Fragments).
  - Level 2: 75 DMG. Gain 1 Fragment per Skill Card in Hand and Discard Pile. (Cap: 2 Fragments).
- **Hermit:** 70 Shield. Target any ally. +1 Bulwark. Next turn, apply 1 Guard. When Guard triggers, gain 1 Fragment per 40 Shield lost (Cap: 3 Fragments).
  - Level 2: 80 Shield.
- **Perch:** 15 DMG. Consume all Energy. Attack all enemies. Multiply ATK by the consumed Energy, then deal +20% DMG per consumed Energy.
  - Level 2: 18 DMG.

### Para a Cauda:
- **Navaga:** Innate. Target any ally. Whenever able, consume 5 Fragment. After 25 Fragments, stop, the target's Cards cost -1 Energy. Can't stack. Exile.
  - Level 2: Innate. Target any ally. Gain 3 Fragment. Whenever able, consume 5 Fragment. After 25 Fragments, stop, the target's Cards cost -1 Energy. Can't stack. Exile.
- **Ranchu:** 20 DMG. Attack all enemies. Apply 3 Vulnerable.
  - Level 2: 25 DMG. Attack all enemies. +1 Bubble. Apply 4 Vulnerable.
- **Shrimp:** 60 DMG. Ambush. Apply 2 Taunt.
  - Level 2: 70 DMG. Ambush. Apply 2 Taunt.
- **Tadpole:** 55 DMG. Ambush. Initial: +1 Jinx to Enemy Draw Pile; otherwise, to Discard Pile.
  - Level 2: 65 DMG.
