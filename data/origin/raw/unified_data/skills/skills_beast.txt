## Cartas de Custo 0:

### Para o Olho:
- **Puppy:** 
  - 5 Heal. Target any ally. +1 Rage. Purify: 1.
  - Level 2: 8 Heal. Target any ally. +1 Rage. Purify: 2.
- **Zeal:** 
  - Initial: pick a Beast Card from Discard Pile; otherwise, from Draw Pile. Put it on top of Draw Pile. Banish.
  - Level 2: Pick a Beast Card from Draw Pile or Discard Pile. Put it on top of Draw Pile. Banish.

### Para a Boca:
- **Confident:** 
  - 5 Heal. Target any ally. Cleanse: 2 & +1 Rage.
  - Level 2: 8 Heal. Target any ally. Cleanse: 3 & +1 Rage.

### Para o Orelha:
- **Innocent Lamb:** 
  - On your next turn, +1 copy of this Axie's other Skill / Secret Cards to Hand. Banish.
  - Level 2: On your next turn, +1 copy with 10% bonus Stat of this Axie's other Skill / Secret Cards to Hand. Banish.

### Para a Cauda:
- **Cottontail:** 
  - 8 Heal. Retain. Target any ally. Consume up to 8 Fragment then Heal 4 HP for each. If 8 are consumed, gain 1 Energy. Unstable.
  - Level 2: 8 Heal. Retain. Target any ally. Consume up to 8 Fragment then Heal 4 HP for each. If 8 are consumed, gain 1 Energy. Unstable.
