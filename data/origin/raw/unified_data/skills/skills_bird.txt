## Cartas de Custo 0:

### Para o Olho:
- **Lucas:** Initial: pick a Bird Card from Discard Pile; otherwise, from Draw Pile. Put it on top of Draw Pile. Banish.

### Para a Boca:
- **Pink Cheek:** If Draw Pile has at least 2 Cards, draw 2 then discard 2. Unstable.

### Para o Orelha:
- **Curly:** 65 DMG. On targets with Fragile, +20 DMG & deal Pure DMG; otherwise, apply 3 Fragile.

### Para o Chifre:
- **Cuckoo:** Target any ally. Apply 5 Cuckoo.

### Para a Costas:
- **Feather Spear:** 30 DMG. Deal 2 hits. +1 Feather. +1 Feather per other Spear card in your Deck to allied Spear card owners.

### Para a Cauda:
- **Cloud:** 8 Heal. Retain. Target any ally. On targets with < 5 Feather, +2 Feather; otherwise, add 1 Feather Dagger to Hand.

## Cartas de Custo 1:

### Para o Olho:
- **Little Owl:** 
  - 20 DMG. Ambush. Target the closest or furthest enemy. Deal 1 hit. This Axie's Little Owl Cards get +1 Hit. (Cap: +5 Hits)
  - Level 2: 20 DMG. Ambush. Deal 2 hit. This Axie's Little Owl Cards get +1 Hit. (Cap: +5 Hits)

### Para a Boca:
- **Doubletalk:** 
  - 25 DMG. Randomly deal 2 hits. Add 1 Jinx to Enemy Discard Pile per unique Enemy hit.
  - Level 2: 30 DMG. Randomly deal 2 hits. Add 1 Jinx to Enemy Discard Pile per unique Enemy hit.
- **Hungry Bird:** 
  - 60 DMG. Ambush. Purify: 1. If target isn't Alert, Steal 10 HP.
  - Level 2: 70 DMG. Purify: 1. If target isn't Alert, Steal 15 HP.

### Para o Orelha:
- **Hungry Bird:** 
  - 60 DMG. Ambush. Purify: 1. If target isn't Alert, Steal 10 HP.
  - Level 2: 70 DMG. Purify: 1. If target isn't Alert, Steal 15 HP.
- **Peace Maker:** 
  - 65 DMG. Target any enemy. On targets with Weak, +20 DMG; otherwise, apply 4 Weak.
  - Level 2: 75 DMG. Target any enemy. On targets with Weak, +20 DMG; otherwise, apply 4 Weak.

### Para o Chifre:
- **Peace Maker:** 
  - 65 DMG. Target any enemy. On targets with Weak, +20 DMG; otherwise, apply 4 Weak.
  - Level 2: 75 DMG. Target any enemy. On targets with Weak, +20 DMG; otherwise, apply 4 Weak.

### Para a Costas:
- **Early Bird:** 
  - 30 DMG. Deal 2 hits. Limit 3: Draw 1 card.
  - Level 2: 35 DMG. Deal 2 hits. Limit 3: Draw 1 card. Bonus 10% Stat to drawn card.

### Para a Cauda:
- **Risky Bird:** 
  - 70 DMG. Initial: +30 DMG and apply 3 Bleed to self.
  - Level 2: 80 DMG. Initial: +30 DMG and apply 2 Bleed to self.
