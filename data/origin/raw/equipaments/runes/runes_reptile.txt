## Runes Reptile

### Rune Types:
- **Defense Rune:** Grants +20 Shield to the user.
- **Resilience Rune:** Increases max HP by 10%.

### Usage:
- Runes can be equipped to enhance Axie abilities.

## Rune Rare:

### Poison Touch:
Whenever hit, apply 1 Poison to the attacker. Take -1.5% DMG per Poison on the attacker. (Cap: -15% DMG).

### Shellshock:
When allied Secrets are triggered, deal 10 Pure DMG to enemy Axies then Heal 10 HP.

### Way of Reptile:
When the battle starts, +2 Spike. When your turn ends, heal 5% Max HP.

## Rune Epic:

### Desert Protector:
Solo. Until Round 5, when enemy turn starts, gain 1 Guard and 20 Shield. When Guard triggers, deal 1 DMG to the closest Enemy Axie per 2 Shield lost.

### Prehistoric Armor:
Solo. When your turn starts, +1 Spike. Spike on this Axie grant +3 Bloodspike. Whenever hit, heal 10 HP.

### Shellshock:
When allied Secrets are triggered, deal 10 Pure DMG to enemy Axies then Heal 10 HP.

### Venom Master:
Solo. Before the battle starts, add 1 Venom Burst to your Deck. When your turn starts, apply 2 Poison to a random Enemy 3 times.

## Rune Mystic:

### Paralyzing Glare:
Solo. Debuff Cards apply 2 Doubt then deal Pure DMG equal to (1 + total number of debuffs on the target) to enemy Axies. (Cap: 20 DMG)

### Regenerator:
Solo. When your turn starts, heal 10% of Max HP and Cleanse: 4.

### Spike Spray:
Solo. When your turn starts, +3 Spike. Whenever hit, take (-1 DMG per Spike and Bloodspike) up to -20 DMG then consume 25% of Bloodspike to deal 3 Pure DMG per Bloodspike consumed to all enemies.
