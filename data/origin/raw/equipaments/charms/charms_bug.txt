## Charms Bug

### Charm Types:
- **Venom Charm:** Deals 5 damage per turn to enemies.
- **Defense Charm:** Grants +10 Shield to the user.
- **Stealth Charm:** Increases evasion by 10%.

### Usage:
- Charms can be equipped to enhance Axie abilities.
