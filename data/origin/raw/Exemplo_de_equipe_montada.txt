# Equipe Montada para Axie Infinity

## 1. Axie 1: Plant
   - **Habilidades:**
     - **Olho:** Cucumber Slice - Heal allies while gaining Leaf.
     - **Boca:** Serious - High damage with Sleep application.
     - **Orelha:** Lotus - Heal and apply Bulwark.
     - **Chifre:** Watermelon - High damage against Sleep targets.
     - **Costas:** Mint - Shield allies and Cleanser.
     - **Cauda:** Shiitake - Summon a Mushroom for extra HP.
   - **Runa:**
     - **Way of Plant:** +2 Leaf at the start of battle.
   - **Encantamentos:**
     - **Plant's Charm of Faith II:** +13 Heal.

## 2. Axie 2: Bug
   - **Habilidades:**
     - **Olho:** Mosquito - Steal HP and apply Poison.
     - **Boca:** Cute Bunny - High damage with Fear application.
     - **Orelha:** Kotaro - Apply Death Mark for extra damage.
     - **Chifre:** Pincer - Shield allies while adding Goo.
     - **Costas:** Beetle Spike - Ambush with high damage.
     - **Cauda:** Leaf Bug - Generate Cocoon and shield allies.
   - **Runa:**
     - **Way of Bug:** +3 Cocoon at the start of battle.
   - **Encantamentos:**
     - **Bug's Charm of Power II:** +9 ATK.

## 3. Axie 3: Bird
   - **Habilidades:**
     - **Olho:** Hungry Bird - High damage with HP steal.
     - **Boca:** Peace Maker - Damage boost against Weak targets.
     - **Orelha:** Curly - High damage with Fragile application.
     - **Chifre:** Little Owl - Ambush with multiple hits.
     - **Costas:** Cloud - Heal allies and generate Feather.
     - **Cauda:** Eggshell - Damage with Eggshell generation.
   - **Runa:**
     - **Way of Bird:** +1 Feather at the start of battle.
   - **Encantamentos:**
     - **Bird's Charm of Power II:** +9 ATK.
